The Promise:

I will travel somewhere new every year for my birthday.

It's really quite simple, and yet, not many people can say they've done it.

Let me preface by saying I wasn't the coolest kid... In fact, I spent my 21st birthday in my hometown (in the middle of nowhere) with my family. We went to a bar and grill in town, met up with my aunt, uncle, and cousins, and I had my first legal drinks in America. Mind you, I'd been living in Ireland for the past year, and I was well above the drinking age there. So I spent my night calmly enjoying my drinks, my aunt trying to force me to drink more and more (which ultimately resulted in her being the drunkest and most obnoxious one at the table).

It was in that moment of embarrassment that I decided I would rather not repeat that night for years to come. And with that decision came the idea to spend my birthday somewhere new each year. And from that idea a promise was born.

As an only child, I've always been close with my parents. When I decided I was going to spend my birthday somewhere new each year, I definitely think my parents were a little sad. We'd never really been apart from each other (except for the year I'd lived in Ireland), and my birthday isn't just about me. But...

This promise is for me.
